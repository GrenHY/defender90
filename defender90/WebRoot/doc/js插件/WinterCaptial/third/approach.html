We are genuine partners of the founding shareholders: we strive to reach the same level of understanding of their businesses and we actively contribute to further growth of portfolio companies by leveraging our experience in capital allocation and business development

We are open to any interesting idea: we look for opportunities across multiple geographies/industries, we work with start-ups just as we work with more mature businesses. We would consider growth capital opportunities as well as providing liquidity for the founding shareholders

Our clients invest long-term: they prefer us to dedicate our time and efforts to a small number of great companies. We are prepared to provide such businesses with our continuous backing on their trajectory to becoming market leaders in their respective segments.
