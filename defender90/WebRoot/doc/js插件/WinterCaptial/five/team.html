The core Winter Capital team assembled in 2008 as part of the Investments Department of Interros Group, one of Russia’s largest and oldest private investment holding companies, established by Vladimir Potanin. The primary focus of the team over the subsequent years was to manage and ultimately advise on the disposal of Interros legacy holdings in technology, media & entertainment, agriculture and other sectors. In July 2015, the team established Winter Capital Advisors, an independent investment advisory business focusing on private equity in Russia and CIS.

At Winter Capital Advisors we believe that having the right team is the single most important factor for success. For us, it’s about having people who share our core values:

  • • A disciplined and detail-oriented decision-making process
  • • A creative and entrepreneurial approach to finding and selecting investment ideas
  • • Honest and transparent communications internally and with our partners

At Winter Capital Advisors we have people who make these values part of their everyday life at work.
